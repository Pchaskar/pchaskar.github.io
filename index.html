About

I support pharmaceutical and biotech organizations in designing and executing hypothesis-driven biomarker strategies across early and late clinical development. With over 10 years of industry experience in dermatology, immunology, oncology and precision medicine, I bridge biology, clinical development and data science to enable clear proof-of-concept decisions and personalized medicine approaches.

Consulting Services

  • Biomarker Strategy Design: Development of project- and study-level biomarker frameworks aligned with therapeutic hypothesis and clinical endpoints.
  • Proof-of-Concept & Mechanism Validation: Definition of pharmacodynamic markers and translational readouts supporting early clinical decisions.
  • Patient Stratification & Precision Medicine: Integration of genomics, transcriptomics and clinical data for response characterization.
  • Biomarker Plan Review & Optimization: Independent review of ongoing programs to improve decision clarity and operational feasibility.
  • Cross-Functional Alignment: Facilitation between Clinical Development, Biometrics, Regulatory and Data Science teams.
  • Regulatory-Ready Translational Narratives: Preparation of integrated biomarker summaries for governance and submission support.
