Strategic Profile

I advise pharmaceutical and biotech organizations on designing biomarker strategies that deliver clear, decision-enabling outcomes in clinical development. My work focuses on transforming biological hypotheses into measurable pharmacodynamic, stratification and tissue-based readouts that enable confident go/no-go decisions, reduce development risk and strengthen program differentiation.

With over 10 years of industry experience across dermatology, immunology and oncology, I have supported Phase II–III programs contributing to regulatory submissions and global approvals. I operate at the interface of Clinical Development, Biometrics, Regulatory and Translational Science to align stakeholders around robust, feasible and impact-driven biomarker plans.

Advisory Focus & Value Delivered

  • Proof-of-Concept Acceleration: Design of hypothesis-driven biomarker frameworks that enable early validation of therapeutic mechanism and reduce costly late-stage uncertainty.
  • Clinical Decision Clarity: Integration of multi-modal biomarkers (genomics, transcriptomics, tissue-based) with clinical endpoints to inform dose rationale, response characterization and patient stratification.
  • Program Differentiation: Identification and advancement of innovative biomarker approaches supporting personalized medicine strategies and competitive positioning.
  • Strategic Alignment: Leadership of cross-functional matrix discussions ensuring biomarker plans are scientifically rigorous, operationally feasible and governance-ready.
