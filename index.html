About

I advise pharmaceutical and biotech organizations on building biomarker strategies that accelerate proof-of-concept, de-risk clinical development, and enable precision medicine decisions.

With over 10 years of experience across dermatology, immunology, oncology, and metabolic disease, I operate at the interface of biology, clinical development, and data science. I help teams translate mechanistic hypotheses into actionable clinical evidence frameworks that support confident go/no-go decisions and regulatory credibility.

My focus is not only on generating data, but on ensuring that biomarker programs meaningfully inform asset strategy, patient selection, and development milestones.
