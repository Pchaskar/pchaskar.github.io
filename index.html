Strategic Profile

I advise pharmaceutical and biotech organizations on designing biomarker strategies that deliver measurable, decision-enabling outcomes in clinical development. My work focuses on translating therapeutic hypotheses into robust pharmacodynamic, stratification and tissue-based readouts that reduce development risk, clarify mechanism-of-action and enable confident go/no-go decisions.

With more than 10 years of industry experience across dermatology, immunology and oncology, I have supported Phase II–III programs contributing to regulatory submissions and global approvals. I operate at the interface of Clinical Development, Biometrics, Regulatory and Translational Science, aligning cross-functional stakeholders around feasible, governance-ready biomarker strategies.

Advisory Focus & Value Delivered

  • Accelerated Proof-of-Concept: Development of hypothesis-driven biomarker frameworks enabling early validation of biological mechanism and reduction of costly late-stage uncertainty.
  • Clinical Decision Clarity: Integration of genomics, transcriptomics and tissue-based biomarkers with clinical endpoints to inform dose rationale, response characterization and patient stratification.
  • Program Differentiation: Identification and advancement of innovative biomarker approaches supporting personalized medicine strategies and competitive positioning.
  • Strategic Alignment & Governance Support: Leadership of cross-functional matrix discussions ensuring biomarker plans are scientifically rigorous, operationally feasible and milestone-ready.
